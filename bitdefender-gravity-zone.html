Living Off The Security Tools (LOTST)

Awareness for Bitdefender GravityZone and similar platforms

⚠️ This page is for awareness and education only. It does not provide attack instructions.

Concept

Security agents like those used in Bitdefender GravityZone are legitimate, trusted, and signed. They are not malware by themselves. However, attackers may exploit the trust model by registering their own accounts in the vendor’s infrastructure and convincing organizations to connect agents to those accounts.

How Abuse Could Look

  • The agent is legitimate and installed correctly.
  • The attacker controls the dashboard account where the agent reports.
  • Victims believe they are monitored by a trusted partner.
  • The attacker can selectively hide their own activity while reporting other attacks to appear legitimate.
  • This creates a false sense of security and long-term stealth.

Defensive Takeaways

  • Verify who controls the monitoring dashboard.
  • Ensure agents are registered under your organization’s account, not a third party.
  • Audit dashboards regularly for anomalies.
  • Treat external offers of “free monitoring” with caution.
