Living Off The Security Tools (LOTST)

Awareness for Sophos Intercept X and similar platforms

⚠️ This page is for awareness and education only. It does not provide attack instructions.

Concept

Sophos Intercept X is a legitimate endpoint protection solution that combines anti‑malware, exploit prevention, and EDR capabilities. Its agents are signed, widely deployed, and trusted. They are not malware. However, attackers may exploit the trust model by registering their own Intercept X infrastructure and convincing organizations to connect agents to it.

How Abuse Could Look

  • The agent is legitimate and installed correctly.
  • The attacker controls the Intercept X dashboard where the agent reports.
  • Victims believe they are monitored by a trusted security partner.
  • The attacker can selectively hide their own activity while reporting other attackers to appear legitimate.
  • This creates a false sense of security and long-term stealth.

Defensive Takeaways

  • Verify who controls the Intercept X dashboard your agents connect to.
  • Ensure agents are registered under your organization’s account, not a third party.
  • Audit dashboards regularly for anomalies and hidden activity.
  • Treat unsolicited offers of “free endpoint protection” with caution.
  • Cross-check reported incidents with independent monitoring sources.
