Living Off The Security Tools (LOTST)

Awareness for Rapid7 InsightIDR and similar platforms

⚠️ This page is for awareness and education only. It does not provide attack instructions.

Concept

Rapid7 InsightIDR is a legitimate cloud-based SIEM and XDR platform, trusted by organizations for detection and response. Its agents and collectors are signed, widely deployed, and integrated into enterprise environments. They are not malware. However, attackers may exploit the trust model by registering their own InsightIDR infrastructure and convincing organizations to connect agents to it.

How Abuse Could Look

  • The agent or collector is legitimate and installed correctly.
  • The attacker controls the InsightIDR dashboard where the data is ingested.
  • Victims believe they are monitored by a trusted security partner.
  • The attacker can selectively hide their own activity while reporting other attackers to appear legitimate.
  • This creates a false sense of security and long-term stealth.

Defensive Takeaways

  • Verify who controls the InsightIDR dashboard your agents and collectors connect to.
  • Ensure InsightIDR instances are registered under your organization’s account, not a third party.
  • Audit dashboards regularly for anomalies and hidden activity.
  • Treat unsolicited offers of “free monitoring” with caution.
  • Cross-check reported incidents with independent monitoring sources.
